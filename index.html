About Left Corner - Bordeaux's Premier Ska & Reggae Band

Left Corner brings the authentic sounds of Jamaica to the streets of Bordeaux, France. With our unique blend of traditional ska, rocksteady, and reggae, we create a vibrant musical experience that pays homage to the golden era of Jamaican music while adding our own French twist.

Formed in 2018, our collective of passionate musicians has been sharing the infectious rhythms and soulful melodies that defined the sound of Jamaica in the 60s and 70s. Our mission is simple: to make you dance, feel good, and connect with the rich musical heritage that continues to inspire generations worldwide.

From Kingston to Bordeaux, Left Corner delivers an energetic live show that transports audiences to the heyday of Studio One and Treasure Isle. Whether you're a longtime fan of the genre or discovering these sounds for the first time, our music will move your feet and lift your spirits.
